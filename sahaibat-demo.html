Built to last — not built on grants
⚙️
Our infrastructure is intentionally lean
SahAIbat runs on a modern serverless stack — Vercel, Supabase on AWS Jakarta, and Twilio WhatsApp. There are no data centres to manage, no engineering team on standby, and no fixed overhead that balloons with scale. The entire platform costs less per month to operate than a single field supervisor's salary. That's not a temporary condition — it's a deliberate architectural choice.
📦
Serverless by design
No idle servers. Cost scales only with actual usage — zero sessions means near-zero cost.
💬
WhatsApp as the channel
No app to build, maintain, or update. Kaders already have the client. Distribution cost is zero.
🌏
Hosted in Jakarta
Data stays in Indonesia on AWS ap-southeast-3 — compliant with UU PDP, close to users, fast.
🛡️
No single point of funding failure
Vinatra is a revenue-generating technology consulting firm. SahAIbat's infrastructure costs are covered by the consulting business — not by a single grant that can end or a donor who can pull out. This means the pilot doesn't disappear when a funding cycle closes. If the impact is real, SahAIbat stays running. The architecture is built to make that affordable.
SahAIbat × 1000 Days Fund

Nothing changes for Kaders.
Everything improves for the data.

1000 Days Fund runs a proven 5-step field model. SahAIbat slots into every step without disrupting any of them — it simply adds clinical intelligence and real-time visibility underneath.

📊
A word on Smart Charts — 1000 Days Fund's flagship tool
The Smart Chart is one of the most elegant pieces of human-centred design in Indonesian public health — under $1 to produce, distributed to 400,000 households, and genuinely trusted by Kaders who've used it for years. SahAIbat is not here to replace it. The Smart Chart stays in the Kader's hand for counselling families. SahAIbat handles the clinical calculation in the background — so the Kader spends more time with the mother, and less time squinting at a growth curve.
Step 1
Train & equip Kaders
COE + Smart Charts + 3+3 sessions/yr
→
Kaders receive Smart Charts and training today. SahAIbat reinforces every session with a live clinical answer — Kaders build real confidence without waiting for the next COE visit.
Step 2
Proactive home visits
4 prenatal + 4 postnatal visits
→
Currently relies on paper records and memory for follow-up scheduling. SahAIbat auto-saves every visit and schedules the next one automatically — no paper follow-up lists needed.
Step 3
Screen & treat
Posyandu growth monitoring
→
Kaders manually look up KMS charts and estimate risk. SahAIbat replaces chart lookup with WHO z-score triage in <90 seconds via WhatsApp. SAM/MAM flagged with instant referral letter.
Step 4
Monitor health
Post-birth tracking through 1000 days
→
No current digital system for child-level growth tracking across the full window. SahAIbat builds individual growth curves automatically across visits. Teams see faltering before it becomes SAM.
Step 5
Strengthen the system
Supervision + monthly performance review
→
Monthly Posyandu data is currently compiled manually from paper. SahAIbat gives supervisors and the 1000 Days Fund team a real-time dashboard — updated after every session, not monthly.

How SahAIbat strengthens your donor case

Every Posyandu session generates structured, timestamped clinical data — SAM/MAM rates, referral outcomes, Kader activity, growth trajectories. This is the kind of evidence-based impact data that institutional donors and grant-makers require. SahAIbat turns every Kader interaction into a data point in your M&E framework — automatically, without any extra reporting burden on your team.
🎯

🔬

Anonymised, structured data suitable for academic publications, policy briefs, and global health grant applications.
